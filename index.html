My favorite extracurricular activity is: Loom Knitting .
To read a Blog post about it, click the icon!

This is a fun and simple project by my favorite YouTuber: Looma Hat

Why Loom Knitting:

  • It has a very low cost of entry (less than $25).
  • It is an ideal choice for beginners (2 basic stitches).
  • It requires minimal dexterity and coordination (suitable for all ages and abilities).

The supplies you need to get strated:

  1. A Loom
  2. A Loom Hook
  3. A skein of Yarn
  4. A sharp pair of Scissors
  5. A Row or Stitch Counter
